My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirit

This is my favourite cartoon. The blending of animation types is unique, the storyline is amazing, and the music is wonderful.

The Creator

A movie that makes you think about existence and the world around you, explodes your brain with cool visuals and sufficiently blows stuff up

Doctor Stone

Super interesting anime. Dr. Stone is one of those sorts of anime which you love even though it's extremely over the top. It's realistic while being unrealistic, funny yet with high stakes.
